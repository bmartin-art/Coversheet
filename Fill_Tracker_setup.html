Fill Tracker

One-click bookmarklet · Brookstone cover sheet

1. Drag this button up to your Chrome bookmarks bar:

📋 Fill Tracker
Drag it onto the bar — don't click it here.

2. Fill out a job on the cover sheet, then click Copy for tracker.

3. Switch to your order-tracker tab and click the Fill Tracker bookmark. It reads the job you copied and types it into the tracker's matching fields.

First time, Chrome may ask permission to read the clipboard — allow it. If a tracker field is left blank, its label just didn't match a keyword yet; tell me the field names and I'll tune it. Nothing is saved automatically — always review, then hit the tracker's own Save.
